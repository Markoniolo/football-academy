In our Football Academy, sports traditions are combined with a modern approach. The high-skill trainers give attentive attitude to young athletes

Basics of the technique:

Flexibility, dexterity
Cognitive abilities
Love for football
Coordination
Communication and individual approach

Philosophy

At this age, children do not perceive complex technical and strength exercises. The training focuses on the development of coordination and the formation of interest in the game. In the classroom, coaches in a game format (the simplest relay races with elements of rivalry and scoring goals) reveal the child's capabilities and what needs to be worked on in the future. The exercises take into account the anthropometric and cognitive abilities of the child, to create a comfortable environment for each player.
